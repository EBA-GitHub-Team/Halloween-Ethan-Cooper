Phantom Phactory Attractions

The Ghostly Tour

A guided tour through the darkened halls and chambers of the factory, with guides sharing spine-tingling stories and legends of the phantoms that inhabit the place.

Séance Room

A room where visitors can participate in a simulated séance to communicate with the spirits, complete with flickering candles, Ouija boards, and eerie sounds.

Haunted Laboratory

An abandoned laboratory filled with mysterious and supernatural experiments, complete with bubbling potions and ethereal effects.

The Labyrinth of Lost Souls

A maze or labyrinth within the factory, where visitors must navigate through twists and turns while being pursued by ghostly apparitions.

Escape Rooms

Themed escape rooms that challenge guests to solve puzzles and escape from the clutches of restless spirits.
